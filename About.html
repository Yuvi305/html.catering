Making History



It all started in 1928. A love story of two immigrants who had worked in service, and began a family catering business in the Adams Morgan neighborhood of Washington, D.C.


95 years later, our team holds their dream alive. We've catered for over 16 presidential inaugurations, countless state dinners and galas, and thousands of weddings, corporate events, and small family gatherings at home.

OUR STORY
